Rent Credit Program: A Path Toward Manufactured Home Ownership

Qualifying residents may earn purchase credits that may be applied toward purchasing an eligible manufactured home, subject to the written Rent Credit Agreement.

To continue earning and retain purchase credits, residents must remain in good standing under their lease. Purchase credits have no cash value, are non-transferable, and are governed by the written Rent Credit Agreement. The Rent Credit Program is not a financing or loan program. Any purchase or financing is subject to separate agreements and approvals, if applicable.
